POWER, SPEED AND SIMPLICITY

As the name and meaning of the platform already indicates, everything including the concept and design language was influenced by the Japanese culture and mythology.

Most of the concept originates from my personal struggles and preferences when it comes to content management and publishing on the web.

I wanted to keep just the essence and minimal set of tools possible, stripping away all the unnecessary fuzz which nowadays comes with most hybrid-like platforms, empowering the user to focus on the lowest possible set of actions and decisions to make.

The content section, as well as the left sidebar are perfectly aligned and set to match the desired and expected user behaviour. Every single interaction is matched by both sections with tweening gestures and indicators based on the action triggered.
